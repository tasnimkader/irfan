About Me

Dr. Mahfuzur Rahman is an Assistant Professor at the Department of Marketing, Comilla University, Bangladesh. With years of academic and professional experience, he is dedicated to advancing the field of marketing through teaching, research, and community involvement.

Teaching Experience

Dr. Rahman has served in various academic positions at Comilla University, BAIUST, and Victoria University of Bangladesh. He has been inspiring students in the field of marketing since 2016.

Courses Taught

  • Undergraduate: Business Communication, Business Statistics, E-Commerce
  • Graduate: Digital Marketing
  • Professional MBA: Global Marketing

Research Interests

Dr. Rahman's research focuses on consumer online behavior, digital marketing, and brand management. He has published in various national and international journals.

Publications

Dr. Rahman has authored several impactful research papers, including studies on service quality, digital marketing evolution, and social media's role in consumer behavior.

Awards

Dr. Rahman has received numerous awards, including merit scholarships and recognition in business case competitions.

Research Projects

He has led multiple funded research projects, including studies on social commerce, digital content marketing, and consumer behavior in emerging markets.

Technical Skills

Proficient in Python, Power BI, SPSS, and Smart PLS. Skilled in data visualization and machine learning applications.

Community Engagement

Active in various initiatives, including volunteering for Bangladesh and the Bangladesh Youth Leadership Center. An advocate for community development and youth empowerment.
